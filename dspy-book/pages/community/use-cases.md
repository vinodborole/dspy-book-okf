# DSPy in Production

DSPy is in production at **Shopify, Databricks, Dropbox, Microsoft AI, Moody's, AWS, Sephora, VMware**, and dozens more.

This list represents companies that have publicly shared their use cases or have provided permission to be included. It reflects a selection of the many industry applications of DSPy currently in production. To add yours, please open a PR.
